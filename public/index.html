FRIENDS

Frameworks, Research and applIcations in complEx Networks with signeD edgeS

FRIENDS is an initiative that promotes the use of signed networks, i.e., networks containing positive and negative links. We provide educational resources, we organize satellite workshops at complex systems and network science conferences, and we have written a literature review on signed networks.
