---
title: "FRIENDS"
---
FRIENDS is a satellite event that focuses on the unique properties of **signed networks**, that is, networks containing positive and negative links. The Netsci 2026 was the third edition of this series, following CCS 2024 and NetSci 2025. Through invited talks and contributed presentations, the satellite covers recent mathematical models, new network metrics, and the implications of this framework across different scientific fields.
