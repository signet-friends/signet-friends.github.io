FRIENDS (a satellite of the CCS 2024) will address the unique properties of signed networks, i.e. networks containing positive and negative links. Between invited speakers and contributions, we will explore the latest mathematical models, novel network metrics, and implications of this framework in diverse scientific fields.

When: 2nd of September 2024

Where: Room 9, University of Exeter Great Hall, Forum, University of Exeter, Exeter.
