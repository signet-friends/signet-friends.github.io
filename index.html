FRIENDS (a satellite of the NetSci 2025) will address the unique properties of signed networks, i.e. networks containing positive and negative links. Between invited speakers and contributions, we will explore the latest mathematical models, novel network metrics, and implications of this framework in diverse scientific fields.

When: 2-3 June 2025

Abstract submission deadline: Feb 18th, 2025

Where: Maastricht, Netherlands
